Barista

We are looking for an energetic and friendly Barista to join our bar!
You'll be responsible for crafting delicious coffee, tea, and smoothie drinks while providing excellent customer service.
Hourly Wage: $11 - $12 per hour

  • Prepare and serve coffee, espresso, tea, and smoothie drinks.
  • Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
  • Engage with customers and provide exceptional service.
  • Restock ingredients and supplies as needed.
  • Follow all health and safety guidelines.

Café Server

We are looking for a Café Server to take orders, serve food, and create a welcoming atmosphere for our guests.
Hourly Wage: $20 - $40 per hour

  • Take customer orders and process payments.
  • Serve food and beverages with a friendly attitude.
  • Ensure tables and seating areas are clean and welcoming.
  • Assist with food prep and stocking supplies.
  • Provide menu recommendations and answer customer questions.
